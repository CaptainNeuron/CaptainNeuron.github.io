Betrayal

This game is inspired by a board game Betrayal at House on the Hill.

A squad enters the cursed old house and the unknown lies ahead.

Background Story of Betrayal

In the 18th century, four young explorers came to an isolated island in the Atlantic Ocean.
They found a strange villa with a great amount of treasure and they ransacked the whole place.
But their fortune did not last. When they returned, their ship sank and they lost their lives.
The story spread and since then, many people have tried to travel to this island but none have return alive.

Now, a doctor, a young traveler, a witch and a Shiba dog make it to the island and the journey begins……

Choose your character

- **Green Hat:** A normal character who is normal in every aspect. - **Doctor:** A pervert doctor who's got a knife where blood keep dripping. - **Shiba:** A Shiba dog can sometimes uncannily talk. - **Witch:** A character whose animation is easy to make since her legs don't move.

## Betrayal

When the game goes on, one of the players will be cursed and become the betrayer. Betrayer’s all abilities will be enhanced and betrayer’s goal is to kill all the rest players. While others need to team up and find the item to remove the curse.

My Story

That is the first game we try to make. I am the producer, the programer and the artist in this project. We built the game based on cocos2dx(a 2d-game engine). The programming part I was responsible for included the role controlling, the fighting part and some basic game code. As for the art part, none of my team mates could do the art stuff, so I said, I will do it. So I make almost all the art resources of this project.

Our team came up with the 4 characters and we want them to be distinctive. Each character has their own strength, including Green Hat whose strenth is his average ability. Strange doctor is a strong character who hides some dark secret. Then I thought who said the character must be a human? So we have a dog. Finally, since I’ve down with making complex animation for the character, I made the last character a witch sitting on a flying carpet.

We actually found some students who were willing to play the game with us and it went well. We laughed a lot. I belive the game really brings joy to other people.
